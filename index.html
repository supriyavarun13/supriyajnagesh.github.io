Project 01

Lead Routing Automation

Sales Cloud
Problem statement

Manual lead assignment was causing 48-hour delays and uneven workloads across the sales team. Reps in high-volume territories were drowning while others sat idle.

What I built

An Apex-based round-robin assignment engine with territory rules and capacity limits. Leads are scored, routed, and assigned in real-time — reducing average response time from 48 hours to under 2 hours.

Salesforce features used
Flow Builder Apex Triggers Custom Objects Assignment Rules Custom Metadata Reports

Project 02

Case Escalation Dashboard

Service Cloud
Problem statement

High-priority support cases were being missed with no visibility into SLA breach risk. Teams had no early warning system, leading to missed SLAs and frustrated customers.

What I built

A real-time dashboard with milestone tracking, automated alerts triggered at 75% SLA usage, and escalation flows that notify managers instantly. SLA breach rate dropped by 60%.

Salesforce features used
Entitlements Milestones Reports & Dashboards Email Alerts Escalation Rules Flow Builder
