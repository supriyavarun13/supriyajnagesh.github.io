About

A bit about me

I'm a Salesforce professional with 7+ years of experience delivering across Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, and Experience Cloud. I've worked as a hybrid developer, administrator, and production support engineer — often across multiple client orgs simultaneously — building automation, resolving production issues, and stabilising systems under pressure.

My background spans custom Apex and Flow development, third-party integrations (DocuSign, AssureSign, Litify Inbox, Docrio, Twilio), CI/CD with Gearset, and data migration — alongside the less glamorous but equally critical work: UAT support, root cause analysis, and stepping into under-documented orgs and making them stable. I take ownership of problems, not just tasks.

I believe in making technical work visible. The portfolio projects and articles on this site are rebuilt in dev orgs to show the work, since real client orgs live behind NDAs — the patterns are drawn directly from real delivery experience. I also mentor aspiring Salesforce admins and am actively building toward Agentforce, with portfolio projects in progress.

Project 01

Sai Dance Academy — Customer Portal

Experience Cloud
What I built

A full-stack customer portal for Sai Dance Academy using Salesforce Experience Cloud. Students can browse classes, enrol online, and pay via Stripe — all from a public-facing LWR website. Built with custom LWC components, Apex REST integration, record-triggered Flows, and a custom data model spanning five objects. Demonstrates end-to-end Salesforce development from requirements gathering through deployment.

Salesforce features used
Experience Cloud LWC Apex REST Flow Builder Stripe Integration Custom Data Model

Project 02

SLA Command Centre

Service Cloud
Problem statement

Sai Dance Academy's support team had no early warning system — SLA breaches only surfaced in retrospective reports, after the damage was already done.

What I built

A fully declarative SLA monitoring system with real-time case health indicators, automated 75% warning alerts, escalation rules that notify managers instantly, and a live Service Cloud dashboard — all five SLA Health states working across 14 test cases.

Salesforce features used
Entitlements Milestones Flow Builder Escalation Rules Reports & Dashboards Formula Fields
